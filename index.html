Hack The Lab

Purpose

Hack the Lab is a hackathon designed to cultivate interest in STEM and help participants explore connections between CS and other sciences while solving problems. Participants will apply programming skills in innovative projects, varying from physics simulations to advances in biotech. This hackathon welcomes grade levels ranging from 8-12 and all programming skill levels from beginners to advanced!

Projects

Participants will be able to create a project using science and technology to solve a problem of their choice. The scale and importance of the topic is up to the participant, and all types of ideas are welcome.

If you aren't sure whether your project fits the requirements or not, check out the requirements, and if you still have questions feel free to ask us!
